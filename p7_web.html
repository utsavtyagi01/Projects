DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING
The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering offers B.Tech, M.Tech and Ph.D courses. As the name suggest, this stream of engineering deals with the research, design, development and testing of electronic and communication equipment. After completing four year engineering degree in ECE, graduates will have strong theoretical foundation, good design experience and exposure to research and development. The stream also strives to solve engineering problems related to Communication Systems, Electronic, Computer and other related industries. The curriculum is designed in such a way to develop the ability among students to synthesize data and technical concepts from applications to product design.
